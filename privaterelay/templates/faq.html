Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the private beta?

First, you’ll need a Firefox account. Then, use your account to sign up for the waiting list. When a spot opens, we’ll send you an email with a link you can use to complete registration and start using Relay.

Is Relay available only in the US?

The site is currently only available in English, but you can use the service anywhere.

What about spam?

We currently use SocketLabs as our Email Service Provider, which enforces industry standard spam controls. We also have the ability to enforce even more strict spam filtering.

What happens if I reply to a relay email?

We don’t yet offer the ability to reply with the relay address. If you try, nothing will happen. We are working on additional features to let you reply with a nice message and make replies actually work.
