Hide your real email address from hackers and spammers

Firefox Relay makes it easy to create aliases, alternative email addresses that forward to your real inbox. Use it to protect your online accounts from hackers and block spam.

How It Works

Protect your personal identity everywhere you use the Firefox browser.

Install the extension

Download the Relay extension for Firefox. When the icon appears on your Firefox toolbar, the extension is installed and ready to use.

Sign in with your Firefox Account

Open the extension and sign in to your Firefox account. Relay will forward messages to the address associated with your account by default, but you can change this when you create each new alias.

Start using Relay

As you browse, the Relay icon will appear in forms that require your email address. You can generate up to 5 aliases. Messages forwarded to you via your alias will have a Relay banner. If an alias is collected in a data breach or is the source of spam and other unwanted messages, you can block messages or even delete the alias from the Relay management page.
